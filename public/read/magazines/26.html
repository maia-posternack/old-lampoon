Cool #

Issue Editor: YMC '21-'22
Art Editor: AMC '23
Publication: December 2021

On the first day, God created the heavens and the earth. “Let there be light,” He said, and there was light. And God saw that it was
good—or, no, ‘good’ wasn’t quite the right word. It was glorious, but in an understated, huh-I-wasn’t-even-trying-to-do-that, kind of way. Light? Sure, God didn’t mind if it was. But if it wasn’t, that was cool too—

Cool! Yes. That was it. ‘Cool’ was the word God was looking for.
