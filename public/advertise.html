Advertise

Want your ad seen by Drew Faust, Conan O’Brien, and thousands of college students who enjoy fine dining and casual cigars? The Harvard Lampoon is a nationally recognized brand with a huge readership in the Boston area, particularly on Harvard’s campus. If you place an ad with us, we can promise that your advertisement will be examined by thousands upon thousands of the best-looking, most intelligent, and least stingy humans ever to have breathed on this planet.

Our magazine is published five times annually and distributed free to all Harvard undergraduates. It can also be found in bookstores, on newsstands, and in the hands of thousands of loyal subscribers across the country. We are pleased to announce that the Lampoon is now available for purchase on Kindle!

The Lampoon offers many other unique and exciting opportunities for advertisers, whether in one of our popular campus or nation-wide parodies, or right here on our freshly revamped website. If you are interested any of these special opportunities, contact the Lampoon to see what’s coming up next. We may be able to offer exclusive advertising in some projects.

Advertisements in the magazine are sold at 1/4, 1/3, 1/2, full, or two-page sizes, but custom sizes may be accepted as well. Ads are printed in color or black and white on 8.5”x11” high quality glossy paper. Proof copies of ads can be made available upon request.

We accept advertisements in most formats, commonly the following: tiff, jpg, pdf, or Adobe Photoshop/Illustrator. We also have a staff of extremely talented artists who can work with you to design and illustrate the advertisement of your dreams.

To learn more about great advertising opportunities with the Lampoon, please give us a call at 617-495-7801, or send an email to advertising@harvardlampoon.com.
